Our Story

We met in college during a mutual friend's birthday party. Ravi spilled his drink on Priya's dress, and instead of getting angry, she laughed it off. That moment sparked a connection that grew stronger with each passing day.

After five wonderful years together, including two years of long-distance while Priya pursued her master's degree abroad, Ravi proposed during a sunset picnic at our favorite park. Of course, we said yes!

Now we're excited to celebrate our love with all of our favorite people - that's you!

Our Timeline

Our journey together through the years

June 2018

We Met

We first met at a mutual friend's birthday party in college. Ravi spilled his drink on Priya's dress, and instead of getting angry, she laughed it off.

August 2019

First Date

Our first official date was at a small Italian restaurant downtown. We talked for hours and realized we had so much in common.

January 2020

Made It Official

After months of dating, we made our relationship official. Priya surprised Ravi with homemade cookies that spelled out "Be Mine?"

September 2021

Long Distance

Priya moved to London for her master's degree. We spent two years making long distance work with lots of video calls and surprise visits.

June 2023

The Proposal

Ravi proposed during a sunset picnic at our favorite park. He hid the ring in a book of love poems he had written for Priya.
