Our Story

John

❤

Jane

John and Jane first met in college during a mutual friend's birthday party in 2015. What started as friendship quickly blossomed into love. After seven wonderful years together, including two years of long-distance while Jane pursued her master's degree abroad, John proposed during a sunset picnic at their favorite park.

John is a software engineer who loves hiking and playing guitar. Jane is a pediatric nurse who enjoys painting and baking. Together they share a love for travel, having visited 12 countries together, and a passion for volunteering at animal shelters.

Their relationship is built on trust, laughter, and mutual respect. They can't wait to start this new chapter of their lives together as husband and wife.
