How We Met

We first met in college during a literature class. John was the TA and Sarah was the student who always asked the most challenging questions. What started as academic discussions soon turned into coffee dates, and the rest is history.

After five wonderful years together, including two years of long-distance while Sarah pursued her master's degree in Paris, we knew it was time to make it official.

Our Journey

June 2016

We met in college during a literature class. John was the TA and Sarah was the student who always asked challenging questions.

December 2016

Our first official date at a cozy coffee shop downtown. We talked for hours and realized we had a special connection.

August 2018

Sarah moved to Paris for her master's degree. We survived two years of long-distance relationship with weekly video calls and monthly visits.

June 2020

Sarah returned from Paris and we moved in together in a small apartment near Central Park.

December 2021

John proposed during a weekend getaway to Vermont, under a starry sky with a surprise fireworks display.

June 2023

Our wedding day! The day we officially become husband and wife.
