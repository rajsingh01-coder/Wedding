How We Met

Our love story began in the most unexpected way. It was a rainy afternoon in Paris, where we both happened to take shelter under the same awning near the Eiffel Tower. John was there for a business conference, while Jane was finishing her art history studies. We started talking about the city's architecture, and before we knew it, three hours had passed. That chance meeting turned into coffee the next day, then dinners, then weekend trips exploring Europe together. Two years later, John proposed during a hot air balloon ride at sunrise over Cappadocia, with the most beautiful landscape spread beneath us. Now, we're excited to begin our forever together, surrounded by the people we love most.

What we love most about each other? Jane loves John's unwavering kindness and how he can make her laugh even on the toughest days. John admires Jane's creative spirit and the way she sees beauty in everything. Together, we share a love for travel, good food, and lazy Sunday mornings with too much coffee. Our relationship is built on mutual respect, endless support, and the belief that life's greatest adventures are better when shared.
